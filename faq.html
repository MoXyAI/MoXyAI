Frequently Asked Questions

What is MoXy?

MoXy is an AI-powered trading assistant designed to help both beginners and experienced traders navigate the complexities of cryptocurrency and stock markets.

How do I use MoXy?

Simply type your question or command in the chatbox, and MoXy will provide you with insights, recommendations, or answers.

Is MoXy free to use?

Currently, MoXy is in Alpha Release and free to use. However, some features may require a subscription in the future.
